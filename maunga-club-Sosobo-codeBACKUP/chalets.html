Kākāpo

Kakapo is Chalet #1.
This is the largest of the chalets, able to accomidate a maximum of 10 adults and 30 children.
It has a large kitchen and dining area.

Chalet Features

Pūkeko

Pūkeko

Pūkeko is Chalet #2.
This chalet is good for large groups, or families wanting a chalet for themselves.
We have a maximum of 6 adults and 15 children that can stay in Pūkeko. This ensures maximum comfort during your stay with us.

Chalet Features

Maximum of 5 adults and 15 children




Size

large groups/families




Kererū

Kererū

Kererū is Chalet #3.
Kerurū is the smallest of our chalets. It is a great option for families, having a maximum adult count of 2, and children 4.



Chalet Features

Maximum of 2 adults and 4 children




Size

Family
